Configure your Banner+.

Thank you for downloading Banner+, and good luck with your W&M Banner registration!

How to use Banner+

Enter your CRNs below.

After you're done, hit save to store your CRNs for registration.

You can edit these CRNs at any time, so feel free to tweak them whenever you want!

Modify your Banner+ settings.

Change the following preferences to have Banner+ run the way you want.


done
 Switch to "Enter CRNs" Tab.

  After entering Banner for registration and selecting your registration term, Banner puts you in the Find Classes tab by default. Banner+ works by entering your CRNs into the Enter CRNs tab, so the tab needs to be switched. If you'd like, Banner+ will automatically switch to the Enter CRNs tab before entering your CRNs.



done
 Automatic Add to Summary.

  After inputting all of your CRNs in W&M Banner, you need to hit the Add to Summary button to move your classes to the Summary section where you can finally submit them. If you'd like, Banner+ will automatically press this button.



done
 Automatic Submit.

  After adding your CRNs to the Summary section, you need to hit the Submit button to actually sign up for your classes. If you'd like, Banner+ will automatically press this button.


done
 Check Conditional Add and Drop.

  According to W&M Banner you should "check Conditional Add and Drop when you want to drop a course from your schedule on the 'condition' that you are able to add a new course to your schedule without error." If you'd like, Banner+ will automatically check this box.


You can test these changes at any time by going to the Banner+ website, so you'll never have to go into registration blind.
